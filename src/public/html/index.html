This website was developed by Sigma Team at the University of Massachusetts Amherst for Computer Science 326: web programming. Shelf is a book tracking social media application. It allows users to find books and add them to their reading list as well as post reviews on books. An additional feature of our application is the abiliity to add friends. Users are able to find other user profiles and add them as their friends. As a friend you will be able to easily view other users reading list and activity. Users can also comment on other uses reviews and activity.
